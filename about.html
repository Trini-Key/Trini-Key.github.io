About Me

A Pre-law hopeful turned United States Marine. Spent 4 years active duty with two tours overseas. Coming up on a major decision in my life, dedicate 4 more year to the Marine Corps. or move onto something else. Ultimately, I decided to change the direction my life was going in. I enjoyed my time as a Marine, I even planned to do 20 years and become a drill instructor. I was fortunate enough to visit many countries during my time for work as well as liberty. I was always into computers since I was a young boy, mostly how they were made and building them myself. I spent many years building pc's and testing them with the numerous computer games that were known as the ultimate benchmark of a good gaming rig.I later developed an enthusiasm to learn how it works. What goes on behind the scenes how does it do what it does. I went to school considering first to get an engineering degree, and so I attended Wake Tech Community college in Raleigh NC. I also considered Commissioning back into the Marine Corps. as an officer. However, my mind changed, and I transferred from Wake Tech Community college to Georgia State pursuing a Computer Science degree. I have developed skills both in and out of School. I now have experience and basic understand in C++, C#, C, Python, HTML & CSS and intermediate skill in Java just to list a few. I have the confidence and ability to learn new languages to increase my skill set. I am pursuing software engineering positions and opportunities, but ultimately, I Would love to get into the Artificial Intelligence and robotics fields.
